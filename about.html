ABOUT ME

A little about me

My name is Anastasiia Saienko, and I believe that the combination of creativity and technology has the power to change the world. I’m 16 years old, originally from Zaporizhzhia, Ukraine, and now I live in Switzerland, in a small but picturesque village called Mühlau. My biggest dream is to become a programmer so I can create something of my own just like this portfolio website you’re reading right now.

Hobbys

I have been playing the violin for 11 years. Two years ago, I graduated from music school, but I still continue to play and even take part in the Jugendorchester Freiamt (Künstlerhaus Boswil) orchestra. In winter, I love snowboarding - I really enjoy riding down red slopes and freestyle tracks. I can also ski, but I like snowboarding much more. My family and friends say that it even suits my character better.

Moving to Switzerland

Very suddenly and unexpectedly, I moved to Switzerland in March 2022 because of the war in my country. Right after arriving, I started learning German from scratch, and my adaptation to the new country went quite smoothly, thanks to the fact that I already spoke English, which greatly helped me communicate with others.
